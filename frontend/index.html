🔬 About This Disease

Wheat Leaf Rust is a fungal disease caused by Puccinia triticina. It manifests as orange-brown pustules on the leaf surface, reducing photosynthesis and grain yield by up to 30% if untreated.

💊 Recommended Treatment

🧪
Propiconazole 25% EC

Apply 1 ml/litre of water. Spray on affected leaves twice at 15-day intervals.

🌿
Organic Alternative

Neem oil spray (5 ml/litre) as a preventive measure. Effective in early-stage infection.
